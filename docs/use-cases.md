# Use Cases

SocialEagle is designed for approved public-page monitoring workflows where the team needs evidence boundaries, not just a summary.

## Marketing Operations

Marketing teams can use the operating model to review public competitor movement, campaign examples, and daily status without mixing observed findings with unresolved coverage.

Good fit:

- competitor page activity review
- campaign movement notes
- creative example collection
- weekly market-readiness checks

## Agency Review

Agencies can use the evidence-first workflow to prepare cleaner client-facing review packs.

Good fit:

- redacted creative libraries
- source links and timestamps
- campaign comparison notes
- account-team handoffs

## Compliance And Operations

Compliance and operations teams can use the safety model to avoid acting on incomplete monitoring.

Good fit:

- visible blocker lists
- fail-closed completion checks
- audit trail language
- review status before publishing reports

## AI-Agent Builders

The Codex skill template is useful for builders who want an agent to inspect monitoring state without leaking credentials or triggering broad browser-heavy work.

Good fit:

- daily safety inspection
- blocker triage
- off-hours diagnostic rules
- publish-safe documentation

## Not A Fit

SocialEagle is not intended for private account monitoring, credential collection, bypassing platform controls, or hiding incomplete coverage.
